ABOUT US

At UM Lanscaping Services, our mission is to blend design, horticulture and ecology with our clients' vision to create innovative, functional and beautiful spaces that inspire a sense of sanctuary and home. UM Landscaping Services is an urban (residential & commercial) landscape design and build firm. Our professional team of landscape designers, horticulturists, urban gardeners and craftsman have over 10 years of experience working. As a design and build firm, UM Landscaping Services makes the commitment to take a project from concept to completion. Every phase of design, construction and installation is carried out with precision and care, keeping the client's vision in mind.
