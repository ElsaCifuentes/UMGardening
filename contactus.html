Shrub Maintenance

All shrubs need pruning for many different reasons: to retain a desired shape; for thinning so that they grow properly; allow for necessary traffic clearance; and prevention or elimination of any pest problems. If that sounds like too much work, call UM Garden Services to find the help that you need with shrubs!

Three Pruning

Tree pruning is another one of those necessary chores that will contribute to overall good-looking landscaping. Pruning of trees is needed for many different reasons: remove dead branches especially large ones that could cause harm if they fell unattended; remove crowding to provide for better growth; and to increase light and air to the important inner part of the tree.
