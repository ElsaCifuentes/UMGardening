Three Pruning

Pruning is an important part of keeping your trees healthy and looking their best, and using proper technique is an integral part of making this happen. An improperly done pruning job can actually harm your tree and leave it vulnerable to disease or decay.
The three-cut pruning method is a great technique to make sure your pruning cuts are clean and where you want them.

Three Pruning

Tree pruning is another one of those necessary chores that will contribute to overall good-looking landscaping. Pruning of trees is needed for many different reasons: remove dead branches especially large ones that could cause harm if they fell unattended; remove crowding to provide for better growth; and to increase light and air to the important inner part of the tree.
