Climbing Rose Joseph's Coat

Climbers are the high risers of the rose world. Ideal for fences, walls, arbors and trellises; they can grow up and over whatever you can tie them to. Cascades of blooms and lots of growth are some of the characteristics of climbers, making them an excellent choice for any garden. Cardinal-red buds have a coral tint, open golden, then gradually turn red again. The Climbing Joseph's Coat Rose climbs 8-10 ft.
